About Me

Hello! I'm Soham Joshi, a passionate motion designer with over three years of experience in bringing static visuals to life. I graduated from Gujarat University in 2023 with a degree in Animation and Design, and I've since honed my skills in various aspects of motion design. My expertise lies in Adobe After Effects, where I specialize in creating dynamic logo designs, engaging motion graphics, and intricate product animations. Additionally, I have a strong proficiency in developing Lottie animations, which are perfect for enhancing user interfaces with lightweight, scalable animations. I believe in the power of motion to tell stories, convey messages, and create memorable experiences. Whether you're looking to add a touch of motion to your brand, animate a product for a presentation, or develop interactive animations for your app or website, I'm here to help bring your vision to life. Feel free to explore my portfolio and see the magic of motion in action. Let's create something amazing together!
